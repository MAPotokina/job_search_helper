# Job Search Helper

A comprehensive job search assistant designed to streamline and optimize your job application process.

## Project Overview

Job Search Helper is a tool that assists job seekers in managing their job application journey by tracking applications, analyzing job listings for visa sponsorship information, and helping customize cover letters for each position.

## Core Features

### 1. Job Application Tracking
- Keep a centralized record of all job listings you've applied to
- Track application status and important dates
- Organize and manage your job search pipeline
- View application history and statistics

### 2. Visa Sponsorship Detection
- Automatically analyze job listings to identify visa sponsorship availability
- Flag positions that explicitly mention sponsorship
- Filter and search jobs based on sponsorship criteria
- Save time by focusing on relevant opportunities

### 3. Cover Letter Customization Assistant
- Help tailor cover letters to specific job listings
- Suggest adjustments based on job requirements
- Maintain multiple versions of cover letters
- Ensure consistency while personalizing content

## Use Cases

- **Job Seekers Requiring Visa Sponsorship**: Quickly identify which companies offer sponsorship opportunities
- **Application Management**: Keep track of where and when you've applied
- **Efficient Cover Letter Writing**: Adapt your cover letter efficiently for each application
- **Job Search Organization**: Maintain a structured approach to your job search
